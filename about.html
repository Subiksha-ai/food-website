Our Story

Discover the passion behind SpiceLeaf

How It All Began

SpiceLeaf was born from a deep passion for authentic Indian cuisine and a desire to share the rich culinary heritage of both South and North India with food lovers everywhere.

Founded in 2010 by Chef Rajesh Sharma, who hails from a family of traditional cooks in Kerala, SpiceLeaf started as a small family-run restaurant with just 10 tables. Chef Rajesh's vision was simple – to create a place where people could experience the true flavors of India, prepared with traditional techniques and the freshest ingredients.

Over the years, we've grown from that small establishment to become one of the most beloved Indian restaurants in the region, but our commitment to authenticity and quality has never wavered.

Every dish at SpiceLeaf tells a story – of ancient recipes passed down through generations, of the diverse regional cuisines of India, and of our ongoing journey to bring you the very best of Indian gastronomy.

Our Hygiene Promise

At SpiceLeaf, we believe that great food begins with impeccable cleanliness and the highest standards of food safety. Our commitment to hygiene is unwavering, and we take pride in maintaining a spotless kitchen environment.

  • Daily sanitization of all kitchen surfaces and equipment
  • Regular health check-ups for all kitchen staff
  • Strict adherence to HACCP (Hazard Analysis Critical Control Point) principles
  • Fresh ingredients sourced daily from trusted suppliers
  • Separate preparation areas for vegetarian and non-vegetarian food
  • Regular quality audits by independent food safety experts

We invite our customers to visit our kitchen and see our commitment to cleanliness firsthand. Just ask our staff for a kitchen tour during your next visit!
