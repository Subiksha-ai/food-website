Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer catering services?

Yes, we offer catering services for all types of events, from small gatherings to large corporate functions. Please contact us at least 48 hours in advance to discuss your requirements and get a customized quote.

Do you accommodate dietary restrictions?

Absolutely! We offer a variety of vegetarian, vegan, and gluten-free options. Please inform our staff about any allergies or dietary restrictions when placing your order, and we'll do our best to accommodate your needs.

What is your delivery radius?

We deliver within a 5-mile radius of our restaurant. For locations beyond this radius, please call us to check if delivery is possible. Additional delivery charges may apply for extended distances.

Do I need to make a reservation?

Reservations are recommended, especially for weekends and holidays, but not required. You can make a reservation by calling us or using the reservation form on our website.
